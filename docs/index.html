About DJ Angerone

Mike Myers, professionally known as DJ Angerone, is internationally acclaimed. Progressive House & Trance DJ & musician

I try to experiment with various music genres in every track that I make.

Since the begining of his career in 2010, he has already released 5 EP's on a range of worldwide known labels (Booster Records, DSTRCT Recordings etc.) and reiceived a number of music awards.
